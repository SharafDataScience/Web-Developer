About Treat & Eat

Founded with a passion for authentic Levantine flavors, Treat & Eat brings the rich traditions of Levantine baking to the local community. Every day, fresh pastries, manakeesh, and a wide variety of traditional desserts—such as baklava and kunafa—are prepared using time-honored recipes and high-quality ingredients. Each product reflects a strong commitment to authenticity, quality, and taste.
